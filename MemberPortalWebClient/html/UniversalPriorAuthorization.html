Drug Search Quick Tips

Medication Search

To search for a medication, enter the partial or full name of the desired medication in the input titled "Name" or enter a medication I.D. such as an NDC number (e.g. 00022036846), into the input titled NDC # or HCPCS code, then click the search button.

Restrictions

If any medications are found, they will be listed within the section named "Medication/Medical and Dispensing Information". Any of the medications listed may be selected, but are subject to the following restriction(s):

1. Only 1 NDC may be submitted per submission

2. Up to 500 search results will be searchable at once. If unable to find the medication searched for, please refine your search criteria, e.g. type more letters that match the desired medication into the "Name" input and try to search again, or check if the medication I.D. entered is correct.

3. NDC and HCPCS may not be submitted at the same time.

Change Medication Type

In order to change medication type, all selected medications must first be removed, this will enable the medication selection radio buttons.

Filters Search Results

In order to quickly search through the results, you can use the "Filter Drug Results" input box. this will dynamically filter through the results based on your text input.

Required Fields

All required fields, denoted by a red asterix "*", must be filled in on each of the selected medications.
