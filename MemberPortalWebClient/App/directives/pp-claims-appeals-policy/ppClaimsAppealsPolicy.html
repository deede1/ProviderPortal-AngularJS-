Claims Processed by IEHP Delegated Entities (Payors):

  • A provider of service should submit disputes directly to the responsible payor in accordance with the payors dispute guidelines for issues pertaining to payment, denial, adjustment, or contesting of a claim, including overpayments, payment rates, billing issues or any other claim reimbursement decision that they believe was unfairly upheld or underpaid.
  • A provider of service may also submit a second level appeal regarding the outcome of a provider dispute to IEHP within 6-months of receipt of the written dispute determination from the Payor. Upon receipt, IEHP will review the appeal to determine the appropriateness of the Payors handling and issue a determination within 45-working days from the receipt of all required information.
  • Providers of service not satisfied with the initial determination by the Payor wherein the determination involves medical necessity or utilization review have the right to appeal directly to IEHP within 60-working days of receipt of the payors written determination. The de novo right to appeal is included in the written determination letter. Any provider wishing a de novo appeal could request such review by submitting a written request to IEHP that includes the written documentation and documentation related to the initial dispute filed with the Payor.
