Form Validation

  • Form validation message will show either on blur from a required element or on form submit.
  • A label preceded by a red asterisk marks a required field:
  • Validation text will be #F44336 red and bold: This field is required or a specific message indicating what is required (See next point)
  • Validation messages must be descriptive e.g. enter 12 digit member I.D. and describe what the user needs to do right, not what the user did wrong
  • Use a DIV with the class .invalid-text after your input field. For example:
    					
					
    Please enter a valid name.
    An NG-SHOW on your invalidation div with an expression of when to display the error is unnecessary because the .invalid-text will automatically search for ng-invalid and ng-submitted in the adjacent form input.
